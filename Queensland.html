Outline

This is a collection of ideas for new rail projects in Queensland. The goal of these ideas is to improve Queensland's rail network.

Brisbane

This covers the area from the QLD/NSW border to the south, Gympie to the north, the ocean to the east, and the Great Dividing Range to the west.

Doomben Line extension

Credit: Furness804

This would involve reopening the railway line between Doomben and Pinkenba, reviving the railway stations at Eagle Farm, Meeandah, and Pinkenba. The Doomben Line would become the Pinkenba Line.

Gold Coast line extension

Credit: Furness804

The Gold Coast Line would be extended from Varsity Lakes to Tweed Heads (in New South Wales), with new stations at West Burleigh, Tallebudgera, Elanora, Tugun, Gold Coast Airport, Coolangatta, and Tweed Heads.
