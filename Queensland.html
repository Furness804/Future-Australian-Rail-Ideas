Outline

This is a collection of ideas for new rail projects in Queensland. The goal of these ideas is to improve Queensland's rail network.

Brisbane

This covers the area from the QLD/NSW border to the south, Gympie to the north, the ocean to the east, and the Great Dividing Range to the west.

Doomben Line extension

Credit: Furness804

This would involve reopening the railway line between Doomben and Pinkenba, reviving the railway stations at Eagle Farm, Meeandah, and Pinkenba. The Doomben Line would become the Pinkenba Line.

Gold Coast line extension

Credit: Furness804

The Gold Coast Line would be extended from Varsity Lakes to Coolangatta, with new stations at West Burleigh, Tallebudgera, Elanora, Tugun, Gold Coast Airport, Coolangatta.

Darling Downs

This covers the area from the Great Dividing Range in the east, Dalby in the west, the QLD/NSW border in the south, and Yarraman in the north.

Darling Downs passenger rail

Credit: Furness804

This would involve restoring a basic level of regular passenger rail services around the Darling Downs. Potential routes would include:

  • Toowoomba - Millmerran

  • Toowoomba - Warwick

  • Warwick - Goondiwindi

  • Toowoomba - Wallangarra

  • Toowoomba - Stanthorpe

  • Toowoomba - Dalby

  • Toowoomba - Gatton
